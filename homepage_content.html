Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the Dead Man's Switch work?

    You set a check-in interval (e.g., every 30 days). If you miss a check-in, we'll send you reminders. If you don't respond to the reminders, the system will automatically grant access to your designated contacts.

  • Is my information really secure?

    Yes. All your documents are encrypted. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Only the people you grant access to will ever be able to see your documents, and only after the Dead Man's Switch is triggered.

  • Can I change who has access to my documents?

    Absolutely. You can change permissions for any document at any time from your dashboard.
